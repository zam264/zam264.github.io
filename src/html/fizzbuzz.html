FizzBuzz

FizzBuzz is one of the most popular interview questions that gets asked. Naturally, it was one of the first problems I wanted try to program and embed into my site.

The premise is that you want to loop through numbers 1-100, numbers divisible by 3 you want to print "Fizz". Numbers divisible by 5 you want to print "Buzz". Numbers divisible by both 3 and 5 you print "FizzBuzz".
