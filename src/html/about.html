About Me

My name is Steven Zamborsky. I live in western Pennsylvania with my wife, our daughter, and our 2 cats. I really enjoy life in this area, I love the seasons, the landscapes and the wildlife that surrounds us.

When I'm not spending time with my family, I'm doing one of my many hobbies (I have too many for my own good). Outside exercise is one of my favorite things, whether it be running, walking or cycling, I find I'm happiest when I'm outdoors. I've ran a few races and have even managed to place in some of the smaller ones. I also enjoy skiing in the winter, although I don't get to go nearly as much as I would like.
